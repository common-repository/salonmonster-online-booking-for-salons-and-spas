Let client's book 24/7. The easy way to add your [salonMonster.com online booking](https://salonmonster.com, 'salonMonster.com online booking') into your Wordpress site.
== Description ==
This plugin makes it easy to add your salonMonster.com online booking into your Wordpress site.

With salonMonster’s easy online booking your clients can book 24/7 so you don’t miss out on potential bookings and clients are saved the inconvenience of voice mail, busy phone lines or being put on hold.

The app automatically sends out email & text reminders so you don’t have to, leaving you more time to focus on what you love, doing hair.

Try the [salon management app from salonMonster.com](https://salonmonster.com, "salon management app from salonMonster.com") for free to see what it can do for you.

== Installation ==

1. Go to your salonMonster.com account and navigate to "Settings" > "Online Booking"
2. Copy your salonMonster web address from that page
3. Open wordpress
4. Click on salonMonster in the menu
5. Paste in your salonMonster booking page address (URL)
6. Save
7. Use [salonmonster] shortcode to add online booking to any page of your wordpress site
